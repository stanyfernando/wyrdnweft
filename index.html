WYRD (pronounced "werd") – Old English for fate, but not the predetermined kind. This is the concept where the threads of old stories create new expressions, shaping what we create today. WEFT (pronounced "weft") – The horizontal threads that get woven through the vertical warp to create fabric. Without weft, there's no cloth – just loose, meaningless strands.

Terms & Conditions / Privacy Policy

Payment
Full payment is required upfront via bank transfer only. After placing your order, you'll need to email or send your payment proof along with your invoice ID to confirm your purchase.

Pre-Orders & Production
All pieces are made-to-order through timed exclusive drops. Once we announce a drop, you have a limited timeframe to place your order. After that period closes, all orders go straight to production with no cancellations possible. Production timelines and delivery dates may vary depending on demand and material availability, but if we're running more than 2-3 weeks late from the promised delivery date, you get a full refund.

Cancellations
You can cancel your order before it's confirmed and sent to production. Once production starts, cancellations aren't possible since everything is printed per order.

Shipping & Delivery
We ship primarily within Sri Lanka. Depending on your location, additional shipping charges may apply - we'll let you know before processing. Any damage during delivery will be handled case by case, usually through courier insurance.

Returns & Refunds
Refunds are available only for customers within Sri Lanka. Items must be returned within 5 days of purchase with original tags and packaging intact. Pieces must be in resellable condition - no stains, odours, or damage. Return shipping costs are your responsibility.

Sizing & Fit
Size charts will be mentioned on the order forms in LK sizing. If you're unsure, contact us via email or Instagram DM before your order is confirmed - we're here to help you get the right fit.

Your Responsibilities
Double-check your delivery address and payment details. We're not responsible for orders sent to incorrect addresses or payments made to wrong account numbers.

Our Rights
We reserve the right to refuse delivery on any order, with a full refund provided only if payment has been made. We may update these terms occasionally - continued use means you accept changes.

Privacy
We collect your info through Google Forms for order processing only. Your details are deleted after delivery completion and we don't sell or share your information with anyone.

Intellectual Property
Our designs draw inspiration from historical and cultural sources. While we respect traditional heritage, our specific interpretations are our creative work.

Life Happens
If aliens invade, natural disasters strike, or the internet breaks permanently, we'll do our best but can't promise miracles. Sometimes the universe has other plans.
